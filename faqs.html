Why Develop a Token?

To put it plain and simple we wanted to create a token so it’s easy for players to get value in or out of the game whenever they want to. In most games, all those hours you have spent, the blood sweat and tears can’t be converted back into cash/crypto. We think it’s only fair to do things differently and enable you to cash out whenever you want. That’s why we created our token the Daba!

How would this work?

Our token the Daba is basically the Dollar/Euro/Yen/Pound of the Galaxy. It’s used to pay for everything you want to purchase in-game. However, Dabas are only being created by playing games or fulfilling certain tasks there is no other way to obtain them. In order to play games and earn Dabas you need to buy Arcade Tokens [our revenue model, we need to eat as well]. When you have the arcade tickets you can start playing games and earn dabas and much more.

What can I do with Dabas?

Dabas are the cash of the Galaxy, so if you want to buy something you’ll need it. The Galaxy is designed in such a way, there will always be demand for Dabas. Depending on your strategy you can make different choices where to spend it on. Some would want to spent it on equipment to improve their gaming performance. Others are more into the Defi and will find ways to earn passive income and buy land plots to farm valuable resources. Or you choose to buy mystery boxes with a chance to win big prizes. The possibilities are endless and rewarding to those who spend their dabas.

How does the Daba Hold its value?

As you could read Dabas are only being created when people play games or fulfill certain tasks [hand in a combination of rare elements]. This would imply that the supply would increase with every game that is being played. And although this is partially true, we came up with a burn mechanism to protect the value of the Daba. Burn Mechanism 1: 75% of paid dabas will be burned! For everything that is purchased in-game: land plots, mystery boxes, equipment, boosters 75% of the paid dabas will be burned. The remaining 25% will be used to keep the ecosystem running smooth: 12,5% will be used to provide liquidity and the other 12,5% will be locked up and put in reserve. We will NEVER sell dabas on open market for the benefit of the team. Next to that we will monitor closely if we can bring the burning % up even further. Burn Mechanism 2: 7% royalty fee from trades in the marketplace will be burned. There will be an in-game marketplace where you can sell all your assets to other players for dabas (or buy). From every sell we will hold 7% royalty which will be burned directly. Burn Mechanism 3: 3% claiming fee for every claim on earnings We will take a 3% fee from every claim players make on their earnings. These 3% will also be burned from the supply.

How to buy Arcade Tokens to play for Dabas?

We are developing what we call a Hybrid model in which players can either buy Arcade Tokens with crypto or fiat. We want to bridge the gap between both worlds and while we are at it educate players on how blockchain might be able to provide a solution for them/ make it easier to collect their winnings. Another advantage is that our potential reach is way bigger than when we would solely focus on crypto.

What is an Arcade Token?

You can see the Galaxy as a gigantic Arcade in which you need Arcade Tokens to play games. Instead of tickets, these games pay-out in our utility token the DaBa or other valuable assets.This is also one of our revenue streams and how we can keep on developing the Galaxy.
