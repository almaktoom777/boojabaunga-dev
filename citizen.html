Citizen Card, Attributes and XP

Players will need to purchase a Citizen Boojabaunga ID card. It will initially come with 2 random attribute slots. Attributes will hold a certain skill, which will bear a benefit now or in the future of Boojabaunga. You can unlock more attributes in the future by spending dabas or with earned experience points. Both can be earned by playing games or doing tasks throughout the galaxy. Your ID will function as a wallet in which all your achievements will be visible, usable and stored.

Attributes

Attributes are basically the core strengths of a certain character, games are designed in such a manner you sometimes need to cooperate with others to win certain challenges. However with everything you do in the galaxy you earn experience points. So over time you might be able to obtain more attributes and master them by spending the XP to level up. You can choose to diversify or become a pure master in a certain skill. You can acquire new attributes by spending dabas, but it will be cheaper (but costs more time) to get them with XP points. Some attributes (and thus skills) will be super rare and hard to come by. You only get 2 random attributes with your citizenship card when minting. You can always try to sell it to another person or buy a new one to see if you get that super rare one!

Experience Points

Briefly mentioned above, but by playing games or doing tasks/ having collected things you get experience points [XP]. Those can be used to level up your attributes. The max level that can be achieved is level 99. Obviously, the benefit of that attribute increases when you level up. It’s an exciting way to develop your character over time to a valuable asset besides earning dabas. [which could be sold on our marketplace]

Bonus Benefits Minting: Emoki Tokens

Every Emoki Token comes with a special element on the back, below there is an example of an Emoki Duck. The corresponding element is “Duckoski” so this is the attribute that will be unlocked on the citizenship card. Beside this attribute also the Shroomia attribute will unlock because the Emoki Mint was also to celebrate our partnership with Fantom Shrooms.

Shroom and Shroomies

Everyone who owns a Fantom Shrooms NFT will be able to unlock the Shroomia attribute on their citizenship card. For every Shroomie NFT you own you can get XP to level up the shroomia attribute further, increasing its rewards.
